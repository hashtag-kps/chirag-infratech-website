About Chirag Infratech

16 years of civil construction, four working directors, and a client list built entirely on repeat business.

Our story

From site contractor to Private Limited company.

Chirag Infratech began operations in 2010 as a general works contractor taking on civil construction work across Uttar Pradesh. As the project book grew — highway works, precast structures, institutional buildings — the business was formally incorporated as Chirag Infratech Private Limited in 2018.

Today the company is driven by four working directors with 60+ years of combined experience, delivering ₹406 Cr+ in combined project value across 23 projects for repeat, government-scale EPC clients including L&T Construction, HG Infra and Patel Infrastructure Limited.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To provide construction solutions for major clients on critical national projects — building long-lasting relationships and a reliable reputation that allows for future expansion, with the goal of becoming one of the top contractors in the region.

Our Mission

To procure projects at competitive pricing, provide safe working conditions, and deliver consistent quality — while investing in our team through structured technical and management training.
